Complex Interactive System HW


UML Diagram

The system I am making a UML diagram for is a bee or wasp pollinating flowers. Bees and wasps are actually herbivores gathering food for their offspring. A bee's role in pollination is completely incidental. They're just trying to collect the most pollen, nectar, or floral oils possible. To plants, bees are useful tools in their quest to spread pollen and reproduce.



Flow Chart

In the flow chart, I am mapping out the interactions between the bees and wasps with the flowers.
